How to Install Addon Mod for Minecraft PE

  1. First, open the Mod Skin and Map Provider application on your Android device.
  2. From the main menu, select the "Addon" option to browse the available mods.
  3. Browse through the list of addons and select the one that you want to install on your Minecraft PE.
  4. Once you have selected the addon, click on the "Install Mod" button to begin the installation process.
  5. Wait for the installation process to complete. This may take a few minutes depending on the size of the addon and the speed of your device.
  6. Once the installation is complete, launch Minecraft PE on your device.
  7. In the game, open the "Settings" menu and select "Global Resources."
  8. Look for the newly installed addon in the list of available resources and activate it.
  9. After activating the addon, you can start using it in your Minecraft PE game.

That's it! You have successfully installed an addon mod for Minecraft PE.
